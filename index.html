Our Water Meter


The principle of operation of our device is simple. We have developed a smart sensor(IoT) that counts water flow in time periods and sends data via WiFi to an app on your device. Next, the data is processed in the application and water usage statistics are created. This way you can monitor the instantaneous flow of water. Along with this, you can monitor the amount of water consumed with a decrease in usage, as well as bonuses accumulated for saving water. Bonuses are converted into discounts and cashback from our partners such as coffee shops, banks, clothing and grocery stores, as well as other commercial organizations. Thus, while saving water, you simultaneously invest in protecting our ecology and receive cashback from your daily purchases.
